About Cagwait White Beach

Cagwait White Beach, often hailed as the "Boracay of Caraga," is a crescent-shaped paradise located in the municipality of Cagwait, Surigao del Sur. Its powdery white sand, calm turquoise waters, and lush surroundings make it a perfect getaway for families, couples, and solo travelers seeking tranquility and natural beauty. The beach is known for its serene ambiance, especially during weekdays, offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Visitors can enjoy a variety of activities, from swimming and sunbathing to beach volleyball and picnicking. The local community is welcoming, and the area maintains a charming, laid-back atmosphere, preserving its natural allure. Come and discover the unspoiled beauty of Cagwait White Beach!

Accommodations

Beachfront Cottages

Simple and charming cottages right by the sea. Perfect for those who want to wake up to the sound of waves.

  • Fan or Air-conditioned options
  • Private bathroom
  • Good for 2-4 persons

Resort Rooms

More modern rooms with amenities for a comfortable stay, often part of small resorts near the beach.

  • Air-conditioning
  • Hot & cold shower
  • Cable TV

Guesthouses & Homestays

Experience local hospitality with affordable guesthouses and homestays a short walk from the beach.

  • Budget-friendly
  • Local experience
  • Shared or private facilities

Restaurants & Dining

Local Eateries (Carinderias)

Enjoy authentic Filipino dishes at very affordable prices. Fresh seafood is a must-try!

  • Fresh Catch of the Day
  • Adobo, Sinigang, Kinilaw
  • Open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner

Beachfront Grills

Several stalls offer grilled meats and seafood, perfect for a casual meal with a view.

  • Grilled Fish & Pork Belly
  • Refreshing drinks
  • Sunset dining

Snack & Drink Stalls

Small shops selling snacks, soft drinks, and local delicacies.

  • Chips, candies, ice cream
  • Fresh coconut juice
  • Local pasalubong
